Problem Statement

Navigating post-graduation pathways in the U.S. presents a complex maze for international students, particularly
when it comes to leveraging their F1 visa status to launch and legally operate businesses. Existing solutions often fall short by offering fragmented advice, lacking tailored support for the nuanced scenarios faced by students.

The SEOT emerges as a beacon, providing a cohesive and comprehensive platform. It empowers students with the tools to seamlessly integrate their entrepreneurial aspirations with stringent immigration policies, ensuring their ventures are founded on solid legal ground.

SEOT directly tackles the uncertainties of visa transitions, business establishment, and compliance, addressing the need for a streamlined, informed, and confident approach to visa regulations and self-employment. It’s not just about starting a business; it's about fostering the growth of global talent within the U.S. economy, providing a pathway for students to flourish as contributors and innovators.

The tool’s ambition is to turn the convoluted and often intimidating process into a navigable journey, marking a significant leap over existing platforms that are often impersonal, generic, and detached from the academic objectives of students

User’s Pain Points

After conducting thorough interviews with international students, we've distilled their experiences into core pain points addressed by SEOT:

  • Complexity of Legal Requirements : Students are often overwhelmed by the intricate legal landscape of U.S. immigration, uncertain about how to align their entrepreneurial goals with visa stipulations.
  • Administrative Burdens: Many face the daunting task of handling extensive paperwork and are prone to errors while navigating the bureaucracy of immigration forms and business registration.
  • Academic and Business Misalignment: There is a pronounced difficulty in correlating their academic pursuits with business objectives, a requirement for maintaining their visa status and securing work authorization.

Solution Overview

SEOT introduces an innovative solution that automates critical tasks, ensures regulatory compliance, and offers intelligent business recommendations. Its key features include:

  • Eligibility Assessment: Accurately evaluates F1, OPT, and business eligibility criteria.
  • Business Guidance: Clarifies business structure options and recommends suitable pathways and Create Business on behalf of students.
  • Contract Generation: Automates creation of tailor-made contracts for students’ ventures.
  • DSO Reporting: Ensures accurate and compliant DSO reporting.

Flow of SEOT

  • Evaluate Eligibility: TSEOT processes student information to ascertain eligibility for OPT, utilizing insights from the Copilot system.
  • Qualification Feedback:Unqualified students receive explanations and resources, while qualified students are guided through business structure options like Sole Proprietorship and LLC.
  • Business Creation: Implements tools to gather necessary details for business formation, obtaining EINs, and establishing the student's company.
  • Contract and Compliance: Generates a custom contract aligned with the student's major and facilitates the update of SEVIS through DSOs, ensuring legal compliance.

Development Process

The Self-Employed OPT Tool (SEOT) development harnessed the full capabilities of a modern tech stack, led by the industrious efforts of Full Stack Developer Arun Giri. Anchored in Next.js and Node.js, the SEOT's architecture was meticulously crafted to produce a responsive user interface that could react dynamically to user interactions, and a robust backend to manage the complex logic of immigration law and business compliance processes.

Why Next.js and Node.js?

Next.js was chosen for its server-side rendering capabilities, ensuring a fast initial load and improved SEO, which is critical for user discoverability. Node.js provided a scalable backend framework that could handle multiple requests seamlessly—a must for the diverse international user base SEOT serves.

Key technical Challenges and Resolutions:

  • Challenge: The integration of a complex regulatory framework into a user-friendly application.
  • Resolution:The team implemented a customizable questionnaire, powered by TypeScript, enhancing the app’s ability to process legal jargon into understandable content.
  • Challenge: Automating the creation of contracts specific to each user’s academic and business details.
  • Resolution: By deploying AI-based systems for custom contract generation, SEOT was able to provide personalized documentation, simplifying the DSO approval process.
